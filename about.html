About Doaba Foods

Doaba Food was founded in Jalandhar, Punjab in 2008. Initially we started our company by making wheat flour and distributing it locally. Slowly after getting great feedback and motivation, we started distributing to other cities and states. After the success, we added a lot more products for our customers. We have our own warehouse where we produce and pack the products with proper safety and hygiene measures. Now we took the initiative to take our home product to global markets where our families and friends are residing to get the same exact taste or even better with higher quality products.
